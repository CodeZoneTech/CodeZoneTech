Guptha University

University has the following grading policy:

  • Every undergraduate receives a grade in the inclusive range from 1 to 100.
  • Any grade less than 40 is a falling grade.

Guptha is a professor at the university and likes to round each student's grade according to these rules:

  • If the difference between the grade and next multiple of 5 is less than 3, round grade up to the next multiple of 5.
  • If the value of grade if less than 38, no rounding occurs as the result will still be a failing grade.
  • If the value of grade if less than 38, no rounding occurs as the result will still be a failing grade.

Eg :

  • grade = 73 round to 75 (75-73 is less than 3)
  • grade = 13 do not round
  • grade = 51 do not round (55-51 is more than 3)
Guptha like to have average grade of the undergraduates in the University.
Given the initial value of geade for each of Guptha's undergraduates, write code to find the average of the the undergraduates including the rounding process.
